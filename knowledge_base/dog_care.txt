Topic: Dog Enrichment and Exercise

High-energy breeds like Labradors, Golden Retrievers, and German Shepherds require significant daily exercise to stay healthy and happy. A lack of physical and mental stimulation can lead to boredom and destructive behaviors.

- Daily Walks: Most dogs need at least one 30-60 minute walk per day. For high-energy breeds, two walks are better. A good walk is not just about distance, but also about allowing the dog to sniff and explore, which is mentally stimulating.
- Fetch and Play: Games like fetch are great for burning off excess energy. A 15-20 minute session of vigorous play can be as beneficial as a short walk.
- Puzzle Toys: Food-dispensing puzzle toys are excellent for mental enrichment. They make your dog work for their food, which mimics their natural foraging instincts and can keep them occupied for a long time.
- Training: Regular training sessions, even for just 5-10 minutes a day, are a great way to engage your dog's brain. Teaching new tricks or reinforcing old ones strengthens your bond and provides mental exercise.
